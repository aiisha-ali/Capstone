About me!

My name is Aisha Ali, I am a 23 year old Minnesota native who loves skincare and has found a new love of tech! I wanted to combine these two interests of mine and decided to create Skincare4Us! My goal in creating Skincare4Us is to create a community of fellow skincare lovers and create a platform in which we can leave reviews & suggestions without the reach and influence of modern day capitalism!

Thank you so much for making your way onto my humble site & I really hope you join the community!



My current skin-care favs and skin concerns!

Recently I have been super into retinol, the glass skin trend, slugging, and double cleansing. Some skin concerns I am currently working on are repairing my skin barrier and being consistent with my SPF!
